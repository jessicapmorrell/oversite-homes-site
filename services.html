Services

Four ways to get expert eyes on your project.

From a single document review to full-time oversight, every tier is independent, nationwide, and built to protect your investment.

Tier 01

Instant Gut Check

$59

What you get

  • Expert review of one document set — contractor bid plus accompanying contract, home inspection report, or change order
  • Red flag summary with the top 3 pricing, scope, or risk concerns
  • 48-hour turnaround
  • One round of email follow-up questions

Best for: Homeowners with a document in hand who want a second set of eyes before they sign or pay — any size project, from a single-room refresh to a full renovation.

Start a $59 gut check
Tier 02

Visual Review & Consult

$199

Everything in Tier 1, plus

  • Expanded document review to include architectural plans and project specifications
  • Upload up to 10 photos or videos of the work or property in question
  • 30-minute 1-on-1 video call with the Oversite GC team
  • Written follow-up summary with recommendations, suggested tweaks, and next steps

Best for: Projects already underway; pre-purchase homes with renovation work to price for negotiating or budgeting; or the early design and pre-planning stage of a new renovation.

Book a $199 consult
Tier 03 · Elite

Real-time Oversite

$499/mo

Everything in Tiers 1 and 2, plus

  • Real-time opinions on photos, questions, change orders, and feedback — via text, email, or Slack (your choice)
  • Up to 10 photos or videos reviewed per week
  • Unlimited change order reviews
  • Unlimited text questions during normal business hours
  • Four 20-minute check-in calls per month
  • Month-to-month — cancel anytime

Best for: Pre-construction planning and active construction oversight; investors managing projects remotely; and homeowners with a GC already on the job who want unbiased input — because the only person your contractor answers to should be you.

Start Elite oversight
Tier 04

Custom Project Management & Advocacy

Starting at $1,500

Everything in Tiers 1–3, plus

  • Full bid, contract, plan, and specification review
  • Draw schedule and contractor payment review against work completed
  • Ongoing change order management and negotiation guidance
  • Scheduled virtual FaceTime site walks for real-time visual review
  • Punch list oversight and end-of-project closeout
  • Permit and inspection liaison
  • Monthly retainer for the length of the project — quoted case by case

Best for: Large or whole-house renovations, additions, new builds, historic homes, investors, and out-of-state buyers.
