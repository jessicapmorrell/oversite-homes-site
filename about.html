About

Built by builders. Paid by homeowners.

Oversite exists because the construction industry is opaque by design — and homeowners deserve someone who speaks the language without a conflict of interest.

Oversite Homes was founded by a 25-year veteran of the home building industry who watched, project after project, as homeowners were overcharged, misled, and left in the dark about decisions that cost them tens of thousands of dollars.

We don’t bid on the work. We have no skin in the game other than protecting your investment. That independence is our entire product — and it’s why over 1,000 homeowners have trusted Oversite to be in their corner.

Our practice is paired with a real estate advisory partnership at Engel & Völkers, giving us a rare 360° view: we protect your property investment from both the transaction side and the construction side.
